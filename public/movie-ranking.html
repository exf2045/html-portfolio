The Three Best Movies According to Manny

My Top Three

Stranger than Fiction

I think the idea of finding out that someone has dictated every second of your life, and then meeting "your creator" is wild.

Gladiator

I mean, what a cinematic masterpiece. The soundtrack is truly one of my favorites.

IP Man

Who doesn't love a martial arts movie? IP Man gets me hype everytime I watch it. That fight scene with the 10 black belts close to the end was monstrous.
